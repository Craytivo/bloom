Legacy Makers: Leave Your Mark, Sis!

You are not just blooming. You’re building legacy. The mark you leave matters—in your family, your community, and the lives of those around you.

Jesus left an undeniable mark on the earth, and as His followers, we are called to do the same. Legacy isn’t just about what you build in this life—it’s about what you pass on.

Many of us equate legacy with wealth, but what if legacy is deeper? The prayers you’ve prayed in private, the courage to pursue healing, the faith you’ve fought for—these are your true legacy.

At BLOOM 2026, we’ll gather to reflect, rise, and respond to God’s call to legacy. Explore what it means to leave behind more than material things, and learn how to leave a Godly legacy that echoes through generations.

  • Your Friends
  • Your Children
  • Your Nieces and Nephews
  • Your Community
  • The ones who walk through doors you opened in obedience

Each year, the Lord does something beautiful at BLOOM. This year will be sacred—a moment where women rise not just to bloom but to build.

Sis, the seeds you plant now will become someone else’s harvest. You are not just blooming—you’re building legacy.

The Bloom Sis Gathering

From humble beginnings in 2018 as My Sister’s Keeper, twelve women gathered to discuss Soul Care. It evolved into the Soul Care Retreat, a holistic experience for women to connect.

In 2022, rebranded as Bloom—a safe, nurturing space where Black women connect, share, and grow alongside like-minded sisters, empowered with tools to thrive.
